EMFace for TMJ, explained

It pairs HIFES muscle stimulation with synchronized radiofrequency. HIFES relaxes the overworked jaw muscles that drive clenching and pain, while RF heat reduces inflammation and improves circulation, addressing both the muscular and inflammatory sides of TMD.

Most patients describe a warm, tapping sensation as the applicators work the jaw muscles. It is hands-free and comfortable, you simply relax for about 20 minutes, and you can return to your day immediately afterward.

A typical course is about four sessions spaced 5 to 10 days apart. Dr. Nory will tailor the exact plan to your symptoms and goals during your consultation.

EMFace targets the root muscular and inflammatory drivers of TMD, which can reduce clenching over time. Many patients keep their night guard as protection, and we will advise you based on your results.

EMFace may not be suitable for patients with metal implants in the treatment area, certain electronic medical devices, or who are pregnant. A short consultation confirms whether it is right for you.
